About the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a vast and interconnected franchise created by Marvel Studios featuring superheroes, villains, and storylines across movies, shows, and streaming specials. Starting with Iron Man in 2008, it spans decades of content and storytelling.

What This Site Does

This site helps fans follow the MCU in a clear and logical order. Whether you're a newcomer or a rewatching fan, you'll find watch lists, phase explanations, and tips to enjoy the Marvel universe to its fullest.

Phases of the MCU

  • Phase 1: Origin stories (Iron Man, Thor, Captain America).
  • Phase 2: Expands characters and connects plots (Age of Ultron, Guardians).
  • Phase 3: Culminates in Infinity War and Endgame.
  • Phase 4: Post-Endgame world and multiverse introduction.
  • Phase 5: Ongoing expansion (Quantumania, Guardians Vol. 3, Loki S2).
  • Phase 6: Upcoming final saga (Kang Dynasty, Secret Wars).
